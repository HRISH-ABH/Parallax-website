Dragon Ball is a manga series that follows the adventures of Son Goku as he grows up and trains in martial arts. The series is about Goku's quest to become stronger and his discovery of the Dragon Balls. The Dragon Balls are seven magical orbs that summon the wish-granting dragon Shenlong when gathered.

Black Clover is about Asta, a boy who dreams of becoming the greatest mage in a world of magic. Asta is an orphan who can't use magic, but trains physically to make up for it. Asta and Yuno, another orphan, are abandoned at the same church as children and promise to compete to become the next Emperor Magus. When teenagers, Asta and Yuno are both chosen to become Magic Knights. Asta summons his own grimoire, which has an anti-magic ability, when their lives are threatened.
